✦ Features

Everything you need to manage your stuff

No complicated setup, no bloated software. Just a simple, delightful way to know what you own and who's borrowing it.

📸

Barcode Scanning

Point your camera at any barcode and instantly add books, movies, or games. Cover art and details are pulled in automatically.

📖

Smart Catalog

Your collection, beautifully organized. Filter by type, status, tags, or search for anything. Rate and review as you go.

🤝

Friend Lending

Lend items to friends and always know what's out. Request to borrow from their collections too — no more forgotten loans.

👯

Friend Codes

Add friends with a simple code — no need to share emails or phone numbers. Quick, private, and easy.

🔔

Real-time Notifications

Get instant alerts when someone requests a loan, accepts a friend invite, or returns your stuff. Never miss a thing.

🏷️

Tags & Status

Mark items as owned, in-progress, completed, or on your backlog. Add custom color-coded tags to sort your way.

✦ How It Works

Three steps to a tidy collection

Getting started takes about 30 seconds. Seriously.

1

Sign in with Google

One tap and you're in. No forms to fill out, no passwords to remember. Your account is set up and ready to go instantly.

2

Scan your stuff

Grab a book off the shelf and scan its barcode. We'll look up the title, author, cover art, and all the details. Bulk-scan mode lets you rip through a whole shelf in minutes.

3

Share with friends

Send your friend code to people you trust. Browse each other's collections, request loans, and keep track of everything in one place.
